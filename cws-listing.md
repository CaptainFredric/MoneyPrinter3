## Detailed Description

**ContentForge scores your content before you hit send.**

Ever publish a tweet, LinkedIn post, or Instagram caption and just *hope* it performs? ContentForge removes the guesswork. It analyzes your writing against platform-specific best practices and gives you a clear 0-100 score, a letter grade, and actionable suggestions — all in under 50 milliseconds.

**How it works:**

A floating score badge appears while you compose on X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Threads, and Facebook. The badge updates in real time as you type, color-coded from red (needs work) to green (ready to post). No tab-switching, no copy-pasting into a separate tool. Your score is right there, next to the compose box.

**Popup scoring for 12 platforms:**

Click the extension icon to score content for any platform — even ones without in-page composer injection. Supported platforms:

- X / Twitter
- LinkedIn
- Instagram
- Threads
- Facebook
- TikTok
- YouTube (titles & descriptions)
- Pinterest
- Reddit
- Email subject lines
- Ad copy (Google / Meta)
- General readability (Flesch-Kincaid)

Each platform has its own scoring ruleset tuned to that platform's character limits, formatting conventions, hashtag norms, and engagement patterns.

**A/B Compare:**

Stuck between two drafts? Paste both into the Compare tab and see a head-to-head breakdown. ContentForge picks the winner and shows you exactly why one draft outscores the other.

**AI Rewrite:**

When your score is low, choose a tone — Engaging, Professional, Casual, or Bold — and let AI generate an optimized rewrite. One click inserts the rewrite directly into the platform's compose box. AI is powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash and is strictly opt-in; it never runs unless you ask for it.

**Deterministic scoring:**

This is not an LLM scoring your content. ContentForge uses a heuristic engine — pure rules mapped to each platform's best practices. The same input produces the same score every time. No variance, no hallucinations, no "it depends on the model's mood." Every deduction is itemized so you can see exactly what to fix.

**Works offline:**

If the API is cold or your connection drops, ContentForge falls back to local heuristics built into the extension. You still get a score, a grade, and suggestions — no internet required.

**Privacy-first, no account needed:**

- No signup, no login, no API key
- No data stored on our servers
- No tracking, no analytics
- Open source under AGPL-3.0

ContentForge is built for creators, marketers, founders, and anyone who writes for an audience online. It turns "I think this is good" into "I know this scores 87."
