All 10 Endpoints

Two instant endpoints for zero-latency scoring. Eight AI endpoints for generation and improvement.

POST
/v1/analyze_headline
Score any headline 0–100 with letter grade, power word detection, and specific improvement suggestions.
⚡ Instant
POST
/v1/score_tweet
Score any tweet draft for engagement potential before you post. Checks length, hashtags, emojis, power words, readability.
⚡ Instant
POST
/v1/improve_headline
Submit a weak headline, get back N AI-rewritten versions sorted by score. The analyze → improve cycle.
✦ AI
POST
/v1/generate_hooks
Generate scroll-stopping hook lines for any topic. Choose count and tone (bold, curious, direct, etc.).
✦ AI
POST
/v1/rewrite
Rewrite any text for a specific platform (Twitter, LinkedIn, email, blog) and tone in one call.
✦ AI
POST
/v1/tweet_ideas
Generate a batch of tweet ideas with hashtags for any niche. Never run out of content ideas.
✦ AI

/v1/content_calendar
Build a 7-day content calendar for any niche and platform, with themes and ready-to-post drafts.
✦ AI

/v1/thread_outline

✦ AI

/v1/generate_bio
